I am into adventure anime and dungeon. So I created the game of hunting Minotaurs with four different types of weapons: sword, spear, magic, arrow.

Room 1: Starting Room
The player starts in the "starting room" with default 2 coins in inventory and 2 HP. The player can have option to team up with another person with all sorts of weapon options.

Actions in this room: choose {weapon}, team up with {weapon}, open gate, check inventory, check HP, go south.

Player has to `open gate` to get to "Monster Room".

Room 2: Monster Room
The monster is Minotaurs with 5 HP. It can only be damaged by sword and spear. Each attack cause Minotaurs HP-1. Minotaurs will attack every two actions player make. For example, player enters the room, the player first enter to room and makes an action and after this action, it'll show message: Minotaurs attacking {weapon} next. The person other than player will attack Minotaurs every two player actions. For example, the player first enter the room and make an action, simultaniously this weapon attacks Minotaurs, message shows Minotaurs HP-1. Player has to tell the other person to dodge, the other person only has 1 HP. Can only go to Room 3 after player dodges 1 attacks from Minotaurs. Whenever player leaves the room, Minotaurs's max HP+1, unless it's beaten. Player lose when Hp <= 0.

Actions in this room: attack, dodge, {weapon} dodge, open door, go south.

Room 3: Restuarant
This room is connected with Room 1 and Room 2. The player can user coin to increase HP, and also change weapons. Drink bear causes HP+1 and coin-1, eat meal causes HP+2 and coin-2, eat with princess leads to win. Go back action can only allow player to go back to where they came from.

Actions in this room: change {weapon}, drink bear, eat meal, eat with princess, go back.

Room 4: Treasure Room
Can only enter this room after Minotaurs is beaten. Coin+10. There are two interactives in this room: Princess and treasure box. The player can only choose one between them. If choose treasure box, treasure box become monster and eats the player, player lose.

In this room, go west returns to the Monster Room and can lead to refighting Minotaurs. Go east leads to the Restaurant.

Actions in this room: choose princess, choose treasure box, go east, go west.
